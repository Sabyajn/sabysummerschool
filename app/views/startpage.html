You can now claim funding for your summer school as part of the 2023 summer school programme.

This form can be submitted by any of the following types of school:

  • M academies
  • AP academies
  • R unit
  • S academies

The form can only be submitted by eligible schools with a summer schools’ funding allocation. Payment will be awarded on the basis of actual costs incurred, up to the value of your maximum allocation. (opens in new tab)

You can find the published summer schools guidance on GOV.UK (opens in new tab)

Before you start

Please ensure that you:

  • read the published guidance (opens in new tab)
  • are an eligible institution (opens in new tab)
  • have the details and the 6-digit Unique Number (UNO) of any other schools you delivered a summer school for (you can find UNOs on get information about schools (opens in new tab))
  • have the number of places you delivered – this will include the different number of pupils invited on each day of your summer school
  • information regarding pupils who were invited and attended
  • the activities offered
  • how your summer school was staffed and when it took place
  • any additional cost incurred as a result of running the summer school and any costs you received from parents or carers.
